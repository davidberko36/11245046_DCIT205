Undergraduate Degree Programmes

Here at DCS, we run two programmes, Computer Science and Information Technology. To qualify for Computer Science in Level 200, students must take MATH 121, MATH 123 and DCIT 101.

Computer Requirements

Each student must own a laptop with at least the following specifications:
  • Intel core i3 64-bit CPU
  • 8gb of DDR4 RAM
  • 512gb of SSD
