Why Choose Us?

Choosing to pursue computer science at the University of Ghana offers a unique blend of academic excellence and cultural diversity. The program provides a solid foundation in computer science principles while embracing a vibrant learning environment that celebrates Ghana's rich heritage. With access to top-notch faculty, cutting-edge resources, and a supportive community, studying computer science at the University of Ghana promises a holistic education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for a dynamic and globally relevant career in technology. Please find the admission requirements for both programmes below.

The department runs a bachelor's programme in both Computer Science and Information Technology. Students applying for information Technoloy are admitted straight into the department while students applying for Computer Science are admitted into Mathematical Sciences initially, and then into Computer Science after the first year based on performance.

• Requirements For Admission

Applicants must have taken the WASSCE and obtained an aggregate 10 with at least a B3 in Elective Mathematics.

Applicants must have obtained at least a C4 in Core Mathematics, English, Social Studies, and Integrated Science

Successful students will be admitted into the Mathematical Science Programme and be transitioned into Computer Science based on performance in the first year.

Students must have taken the WASSCE and obtained an aggregate 10 with a minimum of a C4 in Core Mathematics.
