Welcome to the vibrant and innovative Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, where limitless possibilities intersect with cutting-edge technology. Our department prides itself on offering two dynamic undergraduate programs: the BSc. in Computer Science and the BSc. in Information Technology. Through a holistic approach to education, we foster an environment where theoretical understanding meets hands-on experience, equipping our students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology. Join us as we explore the forefront of digital innovation, inspiring the next generation of tech leaders and problem solvers.

• Requirements For Admission

Students must have taken the WASSCE and obtained an aggregate 10 with at least a B3 in Elective Mathematics
