Empowering Women in Cybersecurity: In a pioneering initiative, our department is proudly hosting a certification event aimed at equipping women with the skills and certification to become ethical hackers. The event, tailored to encourage diversity in cybersecurity, will provide comprehensive training and certification opportunities, fostering a more inclusive landscape within the field. Over the weekend, the University of Ghana hosted a successful cybersecurity competition between Computer Science and Computer Engineering students. The event showcased their technical prowess in tackling cyber challenges. Beyond the competition, workshops and talks raised awareness about cybersecurity across the campus community. This event sparked a newfound understanding of the importance of cybersecurity, leaving students and faculty more vigilant and informed about safeguarding digital environments.
